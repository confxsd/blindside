the couples game that actually means something

blindside is a free relationship game where both partners answer the same questions independently — no peeking. Answers are only revealed when you've both submitted. It's honest, surprising, and sparks the conversations you didn't know you needed.

how blindside works

1

pick a question pack

Choose from 30+ packs — date night classics, spicy hot takes, deep relationship questions, or solo self-discovery quizzes like attachment style and love language.

2

answer blindly

Both you and your partner answer the same questions privately. No one can see the other's answers until both are done. Honest answers only.

3

reveal together

Once both of you submit, answers unlock side-by-side. See where you align, where you differ, and let the real conversation begin.
