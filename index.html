Questions

Things people usually ask.

What is Mira, exactly?
A trust-based introduction network. A friend vouches for you, we match you with someone genuinely compatible, and you only meet people who were hand-picked for you.
How is this different from other apps?
You are never browsed by strangers. No feeds, no swiping, no endless scroll. You only appear in front of the one person a friend specifically chose for you.
Do I need a friend to join?
Yes — Mira only works with a real recommendation. If you don’t have someone to vouch for you yet, you can still join the waitlist and we’ll reach out personally.
How often will I get matched?
One carefully chosen introduction per week. No more. We’d rather you meet one right person than a hundred wrong ones.
Will I see photos right away?
No. Character first, photo second. Photos and profiles only unlock after both sides say yes.
Where does Mira launch?
Istanbul first. Other cities will follow, slowly and carefully — one community at a time.
Is it free?
Early access is free. Pricing will come later — and it’ll reflect the care we put into every single introduction.
How is my privacy protected?
You never appear in a public feed. Your profile only exists in front of the one person matched to you. That’s it.
